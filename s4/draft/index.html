Draft Rules: 4 rounds total. Each team picks 2 heroes and 2 aspects. You can choose heroes or aspects freely within constraints.
Group Restrictions (4-group mode): Each team can only draft one hero from each hero group AND one aspect from each aspect group. Second picks must be from opposite groups.
Bot Strategy: Three-stage system - 1) Check round preferences, 2) Check for surprise picks (random), 3) Use strategic selection.
Round Preferences: Bots prefer Hero in R1&R3, Aspect in R2&R4. "Bot Ignore Round Preferences" slider controls chance to draft opposite type.
Hero Priority: Bots use weighted selection from tier list (~38% top choice, ~26% 2nd choice, etc.). Surprise picks ignore tier lists.
Aspect Priority: Leadership always prioritized first (if available), then weighted selection from priority list. Pool aspects ranked last.
Surprise Picks: Completely random selection that ignores both tier lists and Leadership priority. Controlled by separate slider.

Draft-bot order is based on Villain Theory's May 2025 tierlist
